# Sign of Venus Batch

## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz base spirit
- 0.75 oz Cocchi Rosa
- 0.5 oz Luxardo Apricot
- 0.75 oz lemon juice
- 0.25 oz basil syrup

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a clean container
2. Mix thoroughly
3. Label with name and date
4. Refrigerated

## Storage
- Refrigerated
- Shelf life: 30 days
