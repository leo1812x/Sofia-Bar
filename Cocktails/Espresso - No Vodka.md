# Espresso - No Vodka

## Ingredients
- 1L cold brew
- 500mL house amaro
- 8.25oz coffee liqueur

## Preparation
1. Shake, double strain
2. Serve in Coupe
3. Garnish with Coffee beans

## Notes
Non-alcoholic version
