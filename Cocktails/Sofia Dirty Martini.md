# Sofia Dirty Martini

## Ingredients
- 3 oz batch [[Sofia Dirty Martini Batch]]

## Preparation
1. Hard shake, double strain
2. Serve in Coupe
3. Garnish with Cracked pink peppercorn, olive pick

## Notes
NO substitutions of vodka. Can add more olive brine but no available mods for 'light dirt'
