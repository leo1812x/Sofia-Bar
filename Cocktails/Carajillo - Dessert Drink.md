# Carajillo - Dessert Drink

## Ingredients
- 1 oz espresso
- 1 oz dark rum
- 1 oz licor 43

## Preparation
1. Shake, strain
2. Serve in Nick and Nora
3. Garnish with orange peel

## Notes
- Rich dessert cocktail
