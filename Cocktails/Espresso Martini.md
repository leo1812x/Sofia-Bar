# Espresso Martini

## Ingredients
- 3L vodka
- 2L cold brew
- 1L house amaro
- 18.5oz coffee liqueur

## Preparation
1. Shake, double strain
2. Serve in Coupe
3. Garnish with Coffee beans

## Notes
Cold brew must be fresh
