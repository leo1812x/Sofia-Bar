# Lavender Lemonade

## Ingredients
- 2 oz water
- 1 oz lemon juice [[Lemon Juice]]
- 1.5 oz lavender syrup [[Lavender Simple]]

## Preparation
1. Build over pebble ice
2. Serve in Collins glass
3. Garnish with lemon slice

## Notes
- Refreshing non-alcoholic option
