# Mule Keg

## Ingredients
- 3 liter vodka
- 32 oz ginger juice (spicy)
- 80 oz simple syrup
- 80 oz lime juice
- 300 oz water

## Method
Keg and carbonate

## Notes
- Designed for social/group service
- Batch size makes approximately 10 servings
