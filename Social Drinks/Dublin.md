# Dublin

## Ingredients
- 8 oz Tequila
- 8 oz pomegranate juice
- 4 oz grapefruit juice
- 2 oz lemon juice
- 1 spring can

## Method
GARNISH: grapefruit, garnish in dublin glasses

## Notes
- Designed for social/group service
- Batch size makes approximately 10 servings
