# Tamarind Demerara

## Ingredients
- 1:3 Tamarind paste to Rich Demerara syrup

## Method
Cook on stovetop. Bring to boil on high heat then reduce to medium and let simmer for 10-15 minutes

- Shelf life: 30 days
